🎯 Our Mission

To provide every citizen of India — regardless of location, language, digital literacy, or socioeconomic background — with a transparent, accessible, and time-bound mechanism to voice their grievances against government departments and public service providers.

We believe that every complaint is an opportunity to improve governance. GrievAI turns citizen feedback into actionable intelligence for policy reform.

🔭 Our Vision

To make India a zero-grievance nation by 2030 — where every citizen's concern is heard, acted upon, and resolved within stipulated timelines, driven by artificial intelligence and accountable governance.

Why GrievAI?

10 Reasons Why Citizens & Officers Choose GrievAI

GrievAI is not just a complaint box — it is a complete, end-to-end grievance management ecosystem built on modern technology and citizen-first principles.

🤖

AI-Powered Auto Classification

Our Natural Language Processing (NLP) engine reads your complaint, understands the context, and automatically routes it to the correct department — eliminating manual sorting delays. Supports classification in all 22 Indian languages.

📊 94.2% classification accuracy | Average routing time: 3 seconds
🔍

Real-Time Tracking — No Login Required

Unlike traditional systems where you must create an account to track your complaint, GrievAI lets every citizen track their complaint status using just a Complaint ID — anytime, anywhere, on any device.

🕐 Status updated in real-time | SMS alerts at every stage
⬆️

Strict Auto-Escalation Policy

No complaint can be ignored. If an officer fails to act within the stipulated timeline, GrievAI automatically escalates the complaint to the next senior official — Section Officer → Joint Secretary → Ministry Head — without the citizen having to do anything.

⏱️ 30-day maximum limit strictly enforced | 3-tier escalation matrix
🌐

All 22 Scheduled Languages Supported

India speaks in many voices. GrievAI supports all 22 officially scheduled languages of the Indian Constitution's 8th Schedule — including RTL languages like Urdu, Sindhi, and Kashmiri. No citizen is excluded due to language barriers.

🗣️ 22 languages | 3 RTL scripts | Custom Noto fonts for every script

Bank-Grade Security & Privacy

Your personal data and complaint details are protected with 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption, stored on NIC's (National Informatics Centre) Government of India secure Meghraj cloud, and are fully compliant with the IT Act 2000 and upcoming DPDPA 2023 norms.

🛡️ ISO 27001 | VAPT Tested by CERT-In | OWASP Top 10 Secured

Analytics Dashboard for Governance

Administrators and policymakers get access to real-time analytics — complaint heatmaps by district, department performance scorecards, trend analysis, and predictive insights — helping identify systemic governance failures before they become crises.

📈 District-level dashboards | Export to PDF/Excel | Weekly auto-reports

Our Journey

GrievAI — From Inception to National Scale

A timeline of milestones that shaped India's most citizen-centric grievance platform.

🏛️
2019 — Foundation

CPGRAMS 2.0 Initiative Approved

Cabinet Secretariat approved modernisation of the Centralised Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System. AI-integration mandate issued to Ministry of Personnel.

💻
2020 — Development

GrievAI Core System Built

NIC developed the core platform with AI classification engine. Pilot launched in 5 states — UP, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, and Gujarat with 12 departments.

🚀
2021 — National Launch

Pan-India Rollout

GrievAI launched across all 29 states and 8 Union Territories. Mobile app released. SMS-based complaint filing introduced. Toll-free helpline 1800-11-7781 activated.

🌐
2022 — Language Expansion

All 22 Scheduled Languages Integrated

Multilingual NLP model trained on regional corpora. RTL support added for Urdu, Sindhi, and Kashmiri. CSC offline complaint kiosks deployed in 1.2 lakh villages.

📊
2023 — Intelligence Layer

Predictive Analytics & Policy Integration

Complaint heatmaps began informing budget allocation in 8 states. Auto-escalation matrix tightened to 30 days. Satisfaction feedback loop integrated. 2,48,563+ complaints processed.

🔮
2025 — Future Roadmap

Voice Filing, Blockchain & Zero-Grievance Goal

Planned: Voice-based complaint filing in regional dialects, blockchain-based immutable audit trail, WhatsApp integration for rural access, and AI-driven proactive grievance prevention.

Legal Basis

Constitutional & Legal Framework

GrievAI is not a discretionary service — it operates within a robust constitutional and legislative mandate.

⚖️ Constitutional Provisions

  • Article 14 — Right to Equality; no citizen can be denied access to grievance redressal
  • Article 21 — Right to Life includes right to dignified public services
  • Article 39A — Equal justice and free legal aid; access to governance must be universal
  • Article 41 — State obligation to ensure effective public assistance
  • Directive Principles — Good governance as a fundamental state duty

📜 Acts & Policies

  • Right to Information Act 2005 — Citizens can seek information on their complaint status
  • IT Act 2000 & Amendment 2008 — Legal validity of e-complaints and digital records
  • Public Grievances Policy 2023 — 30-day mandatory resolution timeline for all departments
  • DPDPA 2023 — Digital Personal Data Protection Act compliance for citizen data
  • Citizens' Charter Act — Binding service delivery standards for every department
