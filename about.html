🏛️ About GrievAI Portal

AI-Based Citizen Grievance Redressal System — Transparent, Accountable, and Technology-Driven

Our Mission

GrievAI is the Government of India's flagship AI-powered citizen grievance redressal platform, developed under the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions. Our mission is to provide every citizen of India — regardless of location, language, or literacy level — with a transparent, accessible, and efficient mechanism to voice their grievances against government departments and public service providers.

📋 Portal Overview

GrievAI (Grievance Artificial Intelligence) is an advanced e-governance platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to automate the classification, routing, and monitoring of citizen grievances. The portal is built in accordance with the guidelines of the CPGRAMS (Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System) framework.

Launched under the Digital India Initiative, GrievAI aims to eliminate inefficiencies in the traditional grievance redressal process by:

  • Auto-classifying grievances into relevant department categories using AI
  • Providing real-time tracking and transparency to citizens
  • Enforcing timelines and auto-escalating unresolved cases
  • Generating analytics dashboards for administrative oversight
  • Offering multi-lingual support for regional language accessibility

⚖️ Legal Framework

The GrievAI portal operates within the following legislative and policy frameworks:

  • Right to Information Act, 2005 – Citizens can seek information on their grievances
  • IT Act, 2000 – Electronic records and digital signatures are legally valid
  • Citizens' Charter – Service delivery standards and timelines
  • Public Grievances Policy, 2023 – 30-day mandatory resolution timeline
  • National Data Privacy Guidelines – Citizen data protection and confidentiality

💻 Technology Stack

GrievAI is developed using modern, scalable, and secure technologies compliant with Government of India's GIGW (Guidelines for Indian Government Websites) standards:

  • AI/ML Engine – NLP-based complaint classification and priority scoring
  • Backend – Python (Django), REST APIs, microservices architecture
  • Frontend – Responsive HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Database – PostgreSQL with encrypted storage
  • Cloud – Hosted on NIC (National Informatics Centre) Meghraj Cloud
  • Security – SSL/TLS, OTP verification, VAPT tested

Benefits

Benefits of Using GrievAI

⏱️

Time-Bound Resolution

Every complaint is assigned a resolution deadline. Unresolved complaints are automatically escalated to higher authorities.

👁️

Full Transparency

Citizens can view the status, officer assigned, and every action taken on their complaint at any time.

🌐

Pan-India Coverage

Covers all 29 states and 8 Union Territories with state-specific dashboards and reporting.

🗣️

Multi-lingual Support

Interface available in Hindi, English, and 10 regional languages to ensure no citizen is left behind.

📱

Omni-Channel Access

Available via web portal, mobile app, SMS, and helpline — accessible to all citizens regardless of digital literacy.

📊

Data-Driven Governance

Analytics dashboards provide insights into complaint trends, department performance, and policy improvements.
