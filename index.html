Features

Why Choose GrievAI?

An intelligent, transparent, and citizen-first grievance redressal system built for modern India.

🤖

AI-Based Classification

Our AI engine automatically classifies and routes your complaint to the right department within seconds, ensuring faster response times.

🔍

Real-Time Tracking

Track the status of your complaint in real time using a unique Complaint ID — no account or login needed.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Receive SMS and email updates at every stage of your complaint — from registration to resolution.

⬆️

Auto Escalation

Unresolved complaints are automatically escalated to senior officials after the stipulated deadline, ensuring accountability.

🔒

Secure & Confidential

Your data is protected with SSL encryption, stored on Government of India secure servers, compliant with IT Act 2000.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive portal accessible on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile. Available as an app on Play Store & App Store.

Process

How It Works

Simple, transparent, and efficient — file and resolve your grievance in 4 easy steps.

1

File Complaint

Fill the online form with your grievance details. No account required.

2

AI Classification

AI engine classifies and routes your complaint to the right department.

3

Officer Reviews

A dedicated officer reviews and takes action on your grievance.

4

Resolution

You receive notification upon resolution and can provide feedback.
