📋 Privacy Policy

The GrievAI portal is committed to protecting the privacy of all citizens who use this service. This privacy policy describes what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Last Updated: April 2024  |  Effective Date: April 2024

📥 Information We Collect

When you use the GrievAI portal, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Information (provided by you):

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • State or Union Territory of residence
  • Complaint details and supporting documents

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the portal
  • Device type and operating system
  • Session logs for fraud prevention

Information NOT collected:

  • Aadhaar number (unless voluntarily provided for verification)
  • Financial information (bank accounts, card details)
  • Social media profiles or passwords

🎯 How We Use Information

Your personal information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Grievance Processing: Routing your complaint to the appropriate government department
  • Status Notifications: Sending SMS and email updates about your complaint
  • Verification: Confirming complaint ownership for tracking and status enquiries
  • Analytics: Generating anonymized statistical reports for policy improvement (no personal identification)
  • Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access or fraudulent activity
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting requirements under the IT Act 2000 and other applicable laws
✅ We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any private entity or for commercial purposes.

🔐 Data Security

GrievAI implements industry-standard and government-mandated security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS 256-bit Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is fully encrypted
  • NIC Meghraj Cloud Hosting: Data is stored on the National Informatics Centre's Government of India secure cloud infrastructure
  • VAPT Testing: Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing conducted annually by CERT-In empanelled agencies
  • OTP Authentication: Sensitive actions require One-Time Password (OTP) verification
  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures officers can only see complaints assigned to their department
  • Audit Logs: All access and modifications are logged and audited regularly
  • Data Backups: Encrypted backups taken daily with geo-redundant storage
  • Firewall Protection: Web Application Firewall (WAF) deployed to prevent attacks

🍪 Cookie Policy

GrievAI uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used for login session management.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage statistics to improve portal performance (no personal identification).
  • Security Cookies: Used to detect malicious activity and ensure secure connections.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some portal features may not function correctly.

⚖️ Legal Framework & Disclaimer

The GrievAI portal operates within the following legal framework:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 – Governs electronic records, digital signatures, and cybercrime
  • IT (Amendment) Act, 2008 – Data protection and privacy provisions
  • Personal Data Protection Bill – Compliance with upcoming legislation
  • GIGW Standards – Guidelines for Indian Government Websites
  • RTI Act, 2005 – Transparency in grievance handling

Disclaimer: The information provided on this portal is for official grievance redressal purposes only. The Government of India is not responsible for any loss arising from unauthorized use of this portal. All disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in New Delhi, India.

♿ Accessibility

GrievAI is committed to providing an accessible digital experience for all citizens, including those with disabilities. The portal:

  • Complies with WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards
  • Supports screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Offers font size adjustment controls in the top bar
  • Uses high-contrast color schemes for visual accessibility
  • Provides keyboard navigation for all interactive elements
  • Includes alt text for all images and icons

If you experience accessibility barriers, please contact us at accessibility@grievai.gov.in.
