📋 Status Meanings

  • Pending

    Complaint registered, awaiting assignment to department.

  • Under Review

    Officer has received and is reviewing the complaint.

  • In Progress

    Active action being taken to resolve the grievance.

  • Resolved

    Issue resolved. Closure report submitted.

  • Rejected

    Complaint outside scope or duplicate. Reason provided.
